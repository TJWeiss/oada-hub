Operating Standards
Client Engagement
01
Every engagement requires a single Designated Point of Contact. DPOC form signed before any substantive work begins.
02
If the DPOC is not the Transition Principal, require written proof of decision-making authority.
03
All communication is written only. Verbal decisions confirmed in writing before acting.
04
Medications stay with the client or family at all times. Never with vendors.
05
NJ DOH and NY DOH medical clearance forms required where applicable.
Billing & Collections
01
Collect initiation fee before project launch. Non-negotiable.
02
PM fee is 8.5% against the lower of bid or actual — never the higher.
03
Space plan is non-refundable. Make this clear at intake, not at billing.
04
Obtain written client approval before releasing final payment to any vendor.
Vendor Management
01
Insurance certificate required from every vendor before engagement.
02
W9 on file before any vendor receives payment. No exceptions.
03
Vendor Services Agreement must be attorney-reviewed and signed. Currently pending.
04
Intervene immediately on any "this is extra" claims mid-job.
Project File Standards
01
Every client gets a Drive folder: [Last Name], [First Name] -- [Move Date].
02
Folder contains: signed agreements, space measurements/photos, vendor contracts + COIs, master timeline, communication log, move-day runbook, final invoice.
03
Communication log runs concurrently through project close.
